About Us

Sabbouh Mechanical is a family-owned and operated business with over 20 years of experience in commercial refrigeration, heating, and air conditioning. We take immense pride in the quality of our customer service and remain committed to delivering fast, reliable solutions — every time.

We currently serve more than 70 commercial locations across the Greater Toronto Area, and that number continues to grow. Our reputation has been built entirely on word of mouth — a testament to the honesty, dedication, and work ethic that define everything we do.

To support the businesses that depend on us, we offer 24-hour emergency service with fully stocked trucks — ensuring our technicians arrive prepared to get your equipment back up and running without delay. Our expertise spans routine maintenance for rooftop units and all refrigeration systems, as well as urgent repairs when time is critical. In addition to our commercial services, we also provide installation, maintenance, and repair for residential furnaces and air conditioning systems.

Commercial Maintenance

Sabbouh mechanical provides maintenance for our customers to ensure systems are working at maximum efficiency and to prevent these systems/units from breaking down. Refrigeration maintenance includes changing filters for all systems with power washing once a year. Rooftop maintenance includes changing of filters and belts for all exhaust and rooftop units.

Emergency Refrigeration Trailer

Refrigerators and Freezers

Single Door Rentals

Double Door Rentals

Sabbouh mechanical offers emergency refrigeration trailers that are 8x8 feet or 10x8 feet. Trailers are designed for both walk in cooler and walk in freezer applications.

These refrigeration trailers are great for restaurants if they decide to go under renovations and the business is still operational. Also, these trailers can help business owners as well, if they cannot find a part for their walk in cooler or freezer, and are waiting for a few days for it to be repaired. Sabbouh mechanical will provide the service of dropping off and pick up the trailer for any customer across the GTA.

Any questions about rental costs, please call 905-252-6106
