About Us

Sabbouh Mechanical is a family-owned and operated business with over 20 years of experience in commercial refrigeration, heating, and air conditioning. We take immense pride in the quality of our customer service and remain committed to delivering fast, reliable solutions — every time.

We currently serve more than 150 commercial locations across the Greater Toronto Area, and that number continues to grow. Our reputation has been built entirely on word of mouth — a testament to the honesty, dedication, and work ethic that define everything we do.

To support the businesses that depend on us, we offer 24-hour emergency service with fully stocked trucks — ensuring our technicians arrive prepared to get your equipment back up and running without delay. Our expertise spans routine maintenance for rooftop units and all refrigeration systems, as well as urgent repairs when time is critical. In addition to our commercial services, we also provide installation, maintenance, and repair for residential furnaces and air conditioning systems.

Commercial Maintenance

Most failures are preventable; they are simply never caught in time. We build maintenance around your actual operating load—documented filter and coil care, belt and motor wear checks, and performance baselines—so efficiency stays where it belongs, warranties stay intact, and small issues never become lost weekends or lost inventory.

What We Cover:
  • Filter replacement for all refrigeration & HVAC systems
  • Annual power washing of condenser coils
  • Belt replacement for exhaust & rooftop units
  • Full system inspections & performance checks

Ask about our scheduled maintenance plans tailored to your business.

Sabbouh Mechanical offers emergency refrigeration trailer rentals in two sizes: 8’ × 8’ and 10’ × 8’. Each trailer is designed to operate as a fully functional walk-in cooler or walk-in freezer, giving you reliable cold storage exactly when you need it.
