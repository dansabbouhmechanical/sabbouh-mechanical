About Us

30 Years of Trusted Service

Sabbouh Mechanical is a family-owned and operated company with more than 30 years of experience delivering expert commercial refrigeration, heating, and air conditioning services. We take great pride in our workmanship and are committed to providing prompt, reliable solutions our clients can count on.

Serving a diverse portfolio of commercial clients throughout the Greater Toronto Area, our business continues to grow through one of the strongest endorsements in our industry: word of mouth. This reputation reflects the integrity, dedication, and craftsmanship we bring to every job.

We understand that downtime is not an option for the businesses that rely on us. That’s why we offer 24-hour emergency service, arriving fully equipped to accurately diagnose the issue and restore your equipment to full operation with minimal disruption.

In addition to our commercial expertise, we also provide professional installation, maintenance, and repair services for residential furnaces and air conditioning systems — bringing the same standard of quality and care to every home we serve.
